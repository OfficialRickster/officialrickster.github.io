About Me & My Work

I am a System Designer specialized in bridging the gap between creative vision and mathematical execution. My expertise lies in designing robust core mechanics, crafting balanced progression curves, and engineering scalable game systems. I use data-driven approaches to tune player experiences, ensuring that every mechanic feels rewarding and purposeful. I am driven by the challenge of transforming complex design problems into elegant, invisible systems that players simply enjoy.
